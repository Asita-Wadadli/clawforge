Strategy intake

Start with clarity.
Tell us what pressure you are under, what issue you are facing, and what kind of communication support you actually need.

Use this page as your intake guide before the strategy call. If your need is urgent, go straight to Rapid Response Desk. If your situation is broader, this helps map the right package, timeline, and communication architecture.

What to prepare

  • the speech, statement, issue, or controversy
  • your role: campaign, commentator, advocacy group, civic organization, or media operation
  • what response assets you need most
  • how urgent the situation is
  • where the communication will be used: radio, social, press, live commentary, briefing, or community outreach

What we look for

  • how fast the issue is moving
  • whether message discipline is weak or unclear
  • whether you need one response or an ongoing system
  • what content formats matter most
  • whether this is rapid response, content engine, campaign support, or civic education work
