Instead of sending clients to generic "Claw Launcher," you create:
- **Your own branded installer** (e.g., "Claw Forge Installer")
- **Your own branded onboarding** (your colors, logo, domain)

Clients see YOUR brand, not the underlying platform.

## Technical Setup

### Step 1: Get Your Domain
1. Purchase domain (Cloudflare recommended for .com/.net)
2. Or use Namecheap for budget options (.cc, .site, .info)
3. Transfer DNS to Cloudflare for best performance

### Step 2: Create Logo
- Size: 512x512 pixels
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Style: Professional, recognizable
- Use ChatGPT/DALL-E: "Create a [color] [symbol] logo, 512x512, professional business style"

### Step 3: Get Brand Colors
Ask ChatGPT: "What are the main hex colors for [description of your brand style]?"

Example output:
- Primary: #60a5fa (electric blue)
- Background: #0a0f18 (dark navy)
- Accent: #f87171 (coral)

### Step 4: Configure in Agency Launcher
1. Go to Claw Launcher
2. Click rocket ship → Agency Dashboard
3. Click profile → White Label
4. Upload logo
5. Enter your branded name
6. Add your domain
7. Set your colors
8. Save

### Step 5: DNS Configuration
In Cloudflare (or your DNS provider):
1. Add CNAME record: @ → [provided CNAME target]
2. Add CNAME record: www → [same target]
3. Turn off proxy (gray cloud)
4. Wait 30 seconds to 1 hour for propagation

### Step 6: Test
Visit your domain: https://yourdomain.com
Should show your branded installer.
