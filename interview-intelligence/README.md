# Interview Intelligence System

Turn any topic into a complete interview kit in minutes.

## What It Does

Helps journalists, radio hosts, political analysts, and media professionals prepare for interviews by generating:

- **Structured interview questions** (10-20 per topic)
- **5W breakdown** (Who, What, Why, When, Where, Impact)
- **Follow-up logic** (If they say X → ask Y)
- **Talking points** (Key arguments and angles)
- **Show structure** (Intro, segments, closing)

## Example Output

```
═══════════════════════════════════════
INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE KIT
═══════════════════════════════════════

HEADLINE: Economic Recovery Post-Pandemic

QUICK SUMMARY:
• Tourism sector showing 15% growth
• Unemployment still above pre-2020 levels
• Government stimulus programs ending

5W BREAKDOWN:
├─ WHO: Tourism Minister, Hotel Association, Workers
├─ WHAT: Economic recovery trajectory
├─ WHY: Pandemic impact + policy response
├─ WHEN: 2024-2026 critical period
├─ WHERE: Antigua & Barbuda, Caribbean region
└─ IMPACT: Jobs, investment, standard of living

CORE QUESTIONS:
1. Minister, you claim tourism is recovering. What do you say to workers still unemployed?
2. The stimulus programs are ending. What's the plan for those still struggling?
3. Your opponents say this recovery only benefits hotel owners, not workers. Response?
4. What specific metrics will you use to declare 'full recovery'?
5. If recovery stalls in 2025, will you seek new stimulus or let market correct?

FOLLOW-UP LOGIC:
IF "tourism is booming" → "Then why are hotel workers reporting reduced hours?"
IF "we need more time" → "How much more time? What's the deadline?"
IF "opposition is wrong" → "What data proves them wrong specifically?"

TALKING POINTS:
• 15% tourism growth (source: Tourism Authority)
• 2,000 jobs created in Q1 2024
• Hotel occupancy at 78% (vs 65% 2023)
• Counter: Wages stagnant despite growth
```
